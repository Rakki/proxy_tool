Privacy Policy for Proxy Tools

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Proxy Tools is an Android application that helps users route all device traffic or traffic from selected apps through a user-configured proxy connection.

This Privacy Policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, how it is stored, and when it may be shared with third parties.

1. Who this Policy Applies To

This Privacy Policy applies to the Android app Proxy Tools.

For privacy questions, contact:

  • Developer / Publisher: Roboratory
  • Contact email: apps@roboratory.org

2. Data the App Accesses

Depending on how you use the app, Proxy Tools may access the following data:

  • Proxy profile information entered by you, such as proxy host, port, protocol, username, password, routing mode, and selected apps.
  • The list of installed launchable applications on your device, so you can choose which apps should use proxy routing.
  • Runtime connection logs generated on-device, such as connection status, selected routing mode, proxy endpoint, and diagnostic events.
  • Network traffic metadata needed to operate the VPN/proxy tunnel locally on your device.

3. How the App Uses Data

The app uses the above data only to provide its core functionality, including:

  • creating and storing proxy profiles;
  • allowing you to select which installed apps should use the VPN/proxy route;
  • starting, stopping, and maintaining the Android VPN tunnel;
  • routing traffic through the proxy server you configured;
  • displaying connection status, counters, and diagnostic logs inside the app.

4. Installed Apps Information

Proxy Tools accesses the inventory of installed launchable applications on your device only to support the app's core feature of per-app proxy routing.

This information is used locally on-device so you can:

  • view installed apps;
  • search installed apps;
  • select which apps should be routed through the configured proxy.

The app does not use the installed apps list for advertising, profiling, or analytics by the developer.

5. Proxy and Traffic Handling

When you activate a proxy profile, the app creates a local Android VPN tunnel on your device and routes traffic according to the mode you selected.

Important:

  • Your traffic is forwarded through the proxy server that you configure.
  • The developer of Proxy Tools does not operate or control third-party proxy servers entered by users.
  • Because traffic is routed through the proxy endpoint you choose, that proxy operator may process your network data according to its own terms and privacy practices.

You are responsible for choosing proxy providers you trust.

6. Local Storage

The app stores certain data locally on your device using app-local storage, including:

  • saved proxy profiles;
  • selected apps for routing;
  • the currently active profile ID;
  • connection logs and diagnostics.

This local storage is used to preserve app state and improve usability.

7. Advertising and Third-Party SDKs

Proxy Tools includes the Unity Ads SDK to display banner advertising.

When ads are shown, Unity and its advertising partners may collect or receive certain information from your device or app environment, such as device identifiers, IP address, app information, advertising-related events, and similar data needed to provide, measure, and personalize ads, subject to their own policies and applicable law.

Please review Unity's privacy information:

  • Unity Game Player and App User Privacy Policy: https://unity.com/legal/game-player-and-app-user-privacy-policy
  • Unity Google Play data safety guidance: https://docs.unity.com/grow/en-us/ads/privacy/google-data-safety

The developer of Proxy Tools does not control Unity's independent processing of data collected through the Unity Ads SDK.

8. Data Sharing

The developer does not sell user data.

Data may be shared only in the following cases:

  • with Unity Ads and its partners when ads are displayed;
  • with the proxy server or network destination selected by the user as part of the app's core routing function;
  • when required by law, regulation, legal process, or enforceable governmental request.

9. Data Retention

Data stored locally by the app remains on the device until:

  • you delete the data inside the app, if such option is available;
  • you remove or overwrite saved profiles or logs;
  • you uninstall the app, which generally removes app-local data from the device.

Third-party SDKs and proxy providers may apply their own retention policies.

10. Security

The app stores app data locally and uses Android platform mechanisms to operate the VPN tunnel and app storage.

However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure. You should avoid using proxy providers you do not trust and avoid storing sensitive credentials unless necessary.

11. Children's Privacy

Proxy Tools is not directed to children and is not intended for use by children under the age required by applicable law.

12. Your Choices

You can:

  • choose whether to create and store proxy profiles;
  • choose whether to enable all-traffic or selected-app routing;
  • choose which apps are included in selected-app routing;
  • stop using the VPN/proxy feature at any time;
  • uninstall the app to remove app-local data from the device, subject to platform behavior.

You may also be able to manage advertising preferences through your device settings and Unity's in-ad privacy controls where available.

13. Changes to this Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The updated version will be identified by a revised "Last updated" date.
